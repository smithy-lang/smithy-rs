Optimize `Encoder::str()` and `Encoder::blob()` by collapsing multiple `write_all` calls into a single buffer operation. This bypasses minicbor's generic writer to write the CBOR type+length header and payload directly into the underlying `Vec<u8>`, improving performance on serialization-heavy hot paths.
